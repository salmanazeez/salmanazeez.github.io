BT Options

Web Developer

This is a WordPress site developed for BT Options. Previously, company had a very old hard-coded HTML site with less styles and not responsive therefore company wanted to develop a new website. BT Options is a publisher and apart from that they are an authorized Apple products reseller and they have gym for monthly or annual memberships.

Company website had to carry information of the company and what they do yet it has to have the functionality of subscribing to company magazines through online. Giving consideration to what functionality and feature website needed, concluded to build a WordPress site. I picked the Cream Magazine theme for the site and Elementor plugin as a page builder. Pages of the website was build using Elementor page builder. Website had to have the feature of letting the customers to contact the company through a contact form therefore I used Ninja Forms and configured upto the needs.

Subscription part was the hard-pressed phase since it had to contain all the previous subscription details without any lose of data. On the previous HTML website, subscription page was built using PHP and transactions were bit complicated at times. Company had an old WordPress WooCommerce subscriptions site which was not maintained or was upto date. I disabled the PHP subscription page and enabled the WooCommerce after backing up, updating it accordingly and testing it.

Live site: btoptions.com

Technologies and tools

WordPress, WooCommerce, PHP
